Dev Bootcamp Blog

Welcome to my blog! Every week I have written both a technical and cultural blog on a different topic. Technical blog posts focus on (you guessed it) technical issues, ranging from language specific issues (e.g. objects in Ruby) to decisions or question developers might face. My cultural blog responds to questions about my own personal development as I go through Dev Bootcamp and will hopefully give some insight into my learning process! Dev Bootcamp has supplied the prompts for these posts but I've tried to give them some my own personal flair :)

Enjoy!
